WOT IS INTERACTIVE MEDIA?

  • Interactive media is influenced by the study of how systems work (cybernetics), the concept of self-maintenance and self-reproduction in systems (autopoiesis), and theories about how systems interact and function (system theories).

  • Challenges traditional notions of reason, cognition, perception, memory, emotions, and affection.

  • Related concepts include interaction design, new media, interactivity, human-computer interaction, cyberculture, and digital culture.

  • Encompasses augmented reality and virtual reality.

  • Any interface between the user/audience and the medium can be considered interactive.

  • Not limited to electronic or digital media; examples include board games, pop-up books, flip books, and constellation wheels.

WOT IS THE WWW?

  • An information system that allows documents to be connected to each other through hyperlinks.

  • It was invented by Tim Berners-Lee in 1989 and made available to the public in 1991.

  • It is accessed through web browsers, enabling users to view, access, and interact with various types of content such as web pages, images, videos, and other multimedia resources.

so...the internet?

well...no!

The internet is the overarching network infrastructure that connects devices globally, while the World Wide Web is a specific system of interconnected web pages and resources accessible via the internet.
